What is EcoTek?

Our Mission

EcoTek is a student-led organization founded so that a team of diverse students--ranging from beginners with no experience to advanced with much relative practice--can collectively work together, sharing ideas and set of broad engineering skills to collaborate on large-scale projects that prioritize environmental sustainability. By using principles of engineering and computer science, we transform community challenges into innovative and technical solutions. Alongside helping our communities, we plan to gain recognition through competitive excellence and, ultmately, integrate our sustainable creations directly into our communities.
