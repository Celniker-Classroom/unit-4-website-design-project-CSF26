A Little About Me

Hello, my name is Alarycia Coenen, and I am currently a sophomore at Canyon Crest Academy. I founded EcoTek because of my passion for hands-on engineering and my desire to make a meaningful difference in my community. I wanted to create something that combines innovation, teamwork, and environmental action.

Our Story

For a long time, I was unsure of what I wanted to pursue in the future, but I always knew that I loved building, fixing, creating, and understanding how things work. Once I began exploring engineering, I realized it was a field that genuinely excited me. ...

When the idea of creating an engineering club came up, I saw it as an opportunity to build something inclusive for students of all skill levels. I wanted it to be a place where people could learn, contribute ideas, and work together on projects that could have a real impact.

As I thought about possible directions for the organization, I kept coming back to environmental issues in schools. Food waste is a major problem, and many schools do not have effective composting systems. That inspired the idea for our first major project: a smart compost bin designed to make composting more efficient and informative.
