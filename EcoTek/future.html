Future Goals

EcoTek aims to continue creating projects that combine engineering, innovation, and environmental responsibility. In the future, we hope to grow into a team that not only develops creative ideas, but also applies them in meaningful ways within schools and local communities.

Future Plans

  1. Improve and expand the Waste Wise compost bin project
  2. Develop new sustainability-focused engineering projects
  3. Enter competitions to gain experience and recognition
  4. Build partnerships that help bring our ideas into the community
