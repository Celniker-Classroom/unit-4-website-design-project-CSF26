EcoTek's Future and Structure

A Glimpse of the Future

EcoTek is built on a cycle of annual innovation. Each year, our team will identify a pressing community challenge and develop a dedicated technical solution. Following our success of our 2025-2025 smart compost bin, (more details here about projects) we are currently evaluating local sustainability gaps to determine our new project. Our goal will always remain constant and simple: to engineer accessible and effortless solutions that encourage every member of our community to live more sustainably.
