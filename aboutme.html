A little bit about me

Hello, I'm Alarycia Coenen, a sophomore attending Canyon Crest Academy. I founded EcoTek to merge my passion for hands-on-engineering with a commitment to improve and protect our environment. Driven by the desire to reduce waste and protect our local communities, I created EcoTek as a way to use technical innovation to build a more sustainable future, while providing opportunities for students to apply their skills and bridge the gap between classroom learning and real-world impact.

Our story:

For a long time, my career path felt uncertain as I explored a variety of fields. However, one constant always remained: a deep-seated love for building, creating, and comprehending how things work inside and out. When I first discovered the broadness of engineering, I realized that I had found a subject that was as thrilling as it was impactful. Driven by this passion, I set out to create a space where students of all experience levels could collaborate together, which lead to the founding of EcoTek. In indentifying a mission and overall structure for the group, I looked at our own campus and realized the critical gap in sustainability: abundant food waste. Recognizing that school food waste is a major contributer to climate change, I launched our first initiave, engineering a "smart" compost bin to provide a simple solution to a local problem.
