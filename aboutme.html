Founder:

A little bit about me

Hello, my name is Alarycia Coenen. I am a current high school sophomore attending Canyon Crest Academy. My idea for founding EcoTek came from my love for hands-on-engineering and the feeling of the need to do something to improve our communities to help reduce waste in our beautiful environment. I felt that I needed to take action in at least some way, so, what better way than merge something I would like to pursue as a future career and helping make the world at least marginally a better place?

Our story:

I have never really known what I wanted to do for my future career, I was always drifting between different fields and different ideas, but I knew I had a love for building, fixing, creating, and understanding how things work. Once I had a slight taste of what engineering could be, I allowed myself to dream about a far off future that definetelly seemed more pleasant and thrilling than all the rest. With this in mind, my mom discussed the idea of creating an engineering club, and even though I had no experience yet in any field of engineering, I thought why not make it anyway and band it for all levels of students. Then I thought, what should we do? What will the goals be, what are we going to be working towards? I brainstormed and thought about the environment and how our school and many others do not have effective composting. There is so much food waste that is accumulated by schools that it is also a major contributor to climate issues, so with this in mind, I thought, why not create a smart compost bin?
